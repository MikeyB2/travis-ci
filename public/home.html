What is Cstm Made?

NOT just a Todo List!!!

NOT JUST a Shopping-List

It is a Meal Planner, Recipe Book, and a self filling Shopping-List. It will generate a Shopping-List from your Meal Plan for the week.

  1. First You Register to use Cstm Made. Don't worry it is completely free
  2. Second: Start adding the Recipes, with ingredients, to our recipe page
  3. Then head on over to the Meals page and you can start adding the recipes you just added to your meals for whatever day you want to eat that meal.
  4. After adding a recipe to your meal plan. It will automatically add the ingredients of that recipe to the shopping-list
  5. If you want to add other items to the shopping-list that are not part of a recipe. You can do that too!
