How to Use CSTM Made?

Now that you have access you can start planning you meals for the week!

You should start at the recipes tab to add all the RECIPES you are going to having this week.

After creating your recipes and adding them to the RECIPES tab you should head over to the MEALS tab

On the MEALS tab you can select the recipes you just added to the meal and day of the week that you would like.

After you add the recipe that you want and click add it will add it to your meal planner and also add the ingredients from the recipe to the SHOPPING-LIST!

To view the items on your shopping-list you can click on the SHOPPING-LIST tab to view all the items you have added!

You can add or remove items to the list without adding a recipe to your MEAL planner

Happy planning!
